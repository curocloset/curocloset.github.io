What Curo Closet does

AI-powered wardrobe intelligence that helps you get more from what you own and make smarter decisions about what comes next.

01

Snap to import

Photograph your clothes and AI identifies each piece: category, color, material, season, formality. Build your digital closet in minutes, not hours.

02

Closet score

One number that shows how hard your wardrobe is working. Every item gets a utility score based on wear frequency, outfit versatility, and cost-per-wear. Track it over time.

03

Outfit intelligence

Combinations you didn't know you had, built from what you already own. Filtered by weather, occasion, and what's clean. Your wardrobe is deeper than you think.

04

Smart purchase evaluation

Before you buy, see how a new piece fits your wardrobe. How many outfits it unlocks, whether you own something similar, and its projected cost-per-wear.

05

Wardrobe insights

See gaps in your rotation, items you're underusing, and pieces earning their place every week. The information you need to make better decisions about buying, styling, or letting go.
